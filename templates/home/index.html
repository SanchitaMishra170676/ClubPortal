Web-Development

In this era, when the Internet has captivated a massive portion of world's population, websites have become a part and parcel of our lives. Web Development is undoubtedly a never-dying technology; with consistent improvement and changing trends, it is becoming more and more alluring.

Starting from the basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to complete MEAN/MERN stack development (MongoDB, Express, Angular/ React, Node) the complete journey is full of learning and excitement. Machine-Learning

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) focused on building applications that learn from data and improve their accuracy over time without being programmed to do so. It's the idea to implement generic algorithms to predict the result or tell us something interesting about a set of data without you having to write any custom code specific to the problem.Instead of writing code, you feed data to the generic algorithm and it builds its own logic based on the data.

There is just too much data being produced and without an automated system based on machine learning to learn from such systems, we are nowhere.The future of Machine Learning looks promising as the skilled talent pool for Machine Learning engineers is not yet enough to meet the growing demand for trained professionals. And, we are yet to see the full potential of machine learning beyond self-driving cars, fraud detection devices,or retail trends analyses.

AR/VR

AR/VR is where we build virtual digital experiences that can interact with the real world objects, or make computer aided simulations that take you into a new virtual world. In AR, we understand our environment, then use that data to convey information, on the other hand in VR we take the user into a computer simulated virtual world.

It is one of the trendy tech, and is on its way to overtake the market by mass. We at innogeeks work together to make new creative solutions, use-cases for the real world that leaves an impact. XR ( An umbrella word used for AR/VR/MR ) will be the new “Android” in the coming future, playing a significant role in our life, in the same way smartphones are now a part of our daily life.

Internet Of Things

Ever you have thought that you wake up in morning and a hot cup of coffee is waiting for you. Its cool right? Well technology like this is no longer a dream. With internet of things, many devices in your home can relate to each other and send information across. But do you know what exactly it is?

The Internet of Things (IoT) describes the network of physical objects “things” that are embedded with sensors, software, and other technologies for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with other devices and systems over the internet. IoT wants to connect all potential objects to interact each other on the internet to provide secure, comfort life for human. Common instances for embedded computing devices are MP3 players, MRI, traffic lights, microwave ovens, washing machines and dishwashers, GPS even heart monitoring implants or biochip etc.
